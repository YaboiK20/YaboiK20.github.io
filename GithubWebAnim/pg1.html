Speeding, Shooting Star

By Ke-shaun Davis

I often like to use animation as a medium of freedom and self expression. You can make almost anything in the world where your limits are only your imagination. I felt like nothing could replicate that freedom like a star flying through space with explosive speed, passing by planets.
